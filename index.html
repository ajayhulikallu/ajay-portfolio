Google Ads · PMax

Acer Dotcom Multi-Location Performance Max Scale-Up

Scaled Performance Max campaigns across 10+ Acer Dotcom store locations (Airport, Marina Mall, Mountroad, Velachery, etc.) to deliver sustained high-volume conversions at controlled CPA.

Multiple Acer Dotcom retail stores running isolated, low-performing campaigns with inconsistent bidding, budget cannibalization across locations, and no shared audience intelligence. Conversion volumes were fragmented and CPA was volatile across the network.
Consolidated location-level learnings into a Performance Max framework with shared signals. Applied Target CPA and Target ROAS smart bidding per store tier. Built audience lists from GA4 data including past converters and high-intent visitors, feeding these as audience signals into PMax asset groups.
Set up separate PMax campaigns per location with hyper-local asset groups (images of actual stores, store-specific headlines). Used GTM + GA4 for precise conversion tracking — direction requests, phone clicks, web form submissions. Ran A/B tests on creative assets every 4 weeks, pausing low-performing image/text combos. Applied negative keyword exclusions at account level to prevent wasted impressions on irrelevant queries.

ICB Brewery: Search Campaign for Policy-Heavy Category

Drove awareness and footfall for Indian Craft Brewery through Google Search campaigns in a heavily policy-restricted alcohol/hospitality category.

Alcohol-adjacent brand facing Google Ads policy restrictions on direct promotion. Multiple ad disapprovals, limited targeting options, and the need to drive measurable restaurant/brewery footfall without violating platform policies.

Meta Ads · Hospitality

Meta Full-Funnel for Event & Hospitality Brands

Built and optimized Meta Ads campaigns for hospitality and event clients, improving CTR by 18% through structured creative testing and funnel segmentation.

Campaigns were running with broad targeting and no creative differentiation between cold and warm audiences. ROAS was inconsistent and retargeting audiences were small and poorly structured. Rebuilt the funnel: TOF used broad + lookalike audiences (1–3% from past event attendees), MOF used engagement retargeting, BOF used website visitor + form-opened retargeting with urgency creatives (time-limited offers). Separated ad sets by placement type to control creative formats independently.
Ran structured 3-variant tests: (A) static image with benefit headline, (B) short video testimonial, (C) carousel with USP breakdown. Video content consistently outperformed static by 2.3x on engagement. Identified winning format per audience segment and scaled budget 60% toward top performers, reducing wasted impressions by 35%.
