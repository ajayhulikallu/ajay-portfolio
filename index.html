🇮🇳 India · Search + PMax
Indian Craft Brewery

1,64,367 conversions at ₹4.58 CPA — the lowest across all brands — inside a policy-restricted category.

Lowest CPA across all managed brands
₹4.58
Avg. CPA
The Problem
ICB is an alcohol-adjacent hospitality brand — Google Ads policy restrictions meant multiple disapprovals and zero prior compliant campaign structure. Every intent (brewery, cocktails, dinner, near me) was mixed into one broken campaign. Quality scores were low, spend was wasted.
The Approach
Built 6 intent-segmented Search campaigns — Brewery, Restaurant, Cocktails, Dinner, Lunch, Near Me — each with policy-compliant ad copy focused on the dining experience rather than alcohol. Added a PMax layer for broader reach, keeping creatives within policy guardrails throughout.
What Happened
ICB Search Brewery: 16,926 conv at 11.46% CTR, ₹5.99 CPA. ICB PMax Feb-25: 1,09,923 conv at ₹3.19 CPA. ICB 2026 PMax: 27,487 conv. Total brand CPA of ₹4.58 — lowest across all 8 brands managed.

🇮🇳 India · Performance Max
Acer · Multi-Outlet Network

10+ fragmented locations unified under one PMax system — 1,28,000+ conversions at ₹11.30 CPA.

Multi-outlet PMax architecture
1,28,000+
Conversions
The Problem
10+ Acer Dotcom and partner stores across Chennai & Bengaluru — Airport, T Nagar, Mountroad, Marina Mall, Velachery, VR Mall Annanagar, Adyar, Microtouch, AumTech — all running isolated campaigns with no shared audience signals, high CPC variation, and no creative consistency.
The Approach
Individual PMax per outlet — store-specific asset groups, GA4 past-convertor audience signals fed into each campaign, Target CPA bidding. Negative keyword lists and A/B creative tests refreshed every 4 weeks to maintain signal quality and prevent audience fatigue.
What Happened
Microtouch Aug'24: 3,812 conv, ₹4.73 CPA, ₹0.38 CPC. Airport Nov'24: 3,182 conv, 5.46% CTR, ₹0.25 CPC. T Nagar Oct'25: 3,649 conv, 6.29% CTR. 1,28,000+ total conversions across the network at ₹11.30 brand avg. CPA.

Dell · Dotcom Chennai Network

🌏 APAC · GBP + Google Ads
Lenovo · APAC SPOC

250+ broken listings across APAC turned into a +15% discoverability gain — plus a paid campaign in the Philippines.

Multi-market GBP + PMax
+15%
APAC Discoverability
The Problem
Inherited 250+ Lenovo Google Business Profile listings across Southeast Asia and the broader APAC region — inconsistent NAP data, missing store hours, zero attribute setup, no review response protocol. Listings were suppressing local map pack visibility across markets. Separately, needed to drive awareness to Lenovo's store locator in the Philippines.
The Approach
Full audit of all 250+ listings. Built a GBP standardisation playbook — NAP alignment, keyword-rich descriptions, service attribute setup, monthly photo cadence, and a review response template system. Launched PMax on locate.lenovo.com targeting the Philippines market for store locator awareness and traffic (Dec 2025).
What Happened
+15% increase in local discoverability across APAC markets. GBP standardisation playbook adopted across all 250+ Lenovo locations. Philippines PMax campaign on locate.lenovo.com drove measurable awareness and store locator traffic in the market.
