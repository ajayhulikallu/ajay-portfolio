Retail Search + PMax Google Business Profile

Acer Exclusive Stores — making “near me” work harder.

For Acer’s offline store network, the goal was simple: be the default choice when someone searches for laptops “near me” and convert that intent into actual store visits.

Context

Multiple store partners, inconsistent business listings, and fragmented campaigns meant the brand wasn’t fully visible for high intent local searches.

What I did

  • Re‑structured Google Ads accounts by region and store cluster, separating brand, generic, and competitor terms.
  • Rolled out a unified Google Business Profile structure with naming, categories, and UTM conventions.
  • Launched Performance Max for store goals, feeding in store feeds and location extensions.
  • Built Looker Studio dashboards combining ads, GBP, and in‑store data for monthly reviews.

Financial services Lead generation Search + Social

ICB campaign — scaling leads while protecting CPL.

For ICB, the objective was to drive qualified leads for a financial product, while staying within a strict cost per lead and avoiding spam or low‑quality form fills.

Context

The brand was already running ads, but leads were inconsistent and sales teams were struggling with irrelevant enquiries.

What I did

  • Re‑built campaigns around intent tiers, separating core, competitor, and exploratory keywords.
  • Aligned ad copy and extensions with the exact language sales teams used in their best‑performing pitches.
  • Created landing page variants focused on clarity and trust (benefits, eligibility, FAQs, and strong CTAs).
  • Added negative keywords, form validation, and basic lead‑scoring signals to reduce junk leads.
