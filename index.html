AI in Practice

How I use AI in real campaigns

Practical tools integrated into daily workflow — not automation for its own sake.

ChatGPT · Claude
Ad Copy & Brief Generation
Generate 10–15 headline variants per ad group in minutes. Test against each other in Google Ads, identify winners faster, and iterate on CTR data rather than guesswork.
Claude · Perplexity
Campaign Analysis
Paste campaign CSVs into Claude to surface underperforming ad groups and generate client-ready summaries. Perplexity for quick competitor research without 10 tabs open.
Gamma · ChatGPT
Reporting & Presentations
Draft performance commentary from raw metrics. Convert briefs into structured client decks using Gamma — saving time on presentation preparation.
ChatGPT · Grok
Automation Scripting
Write Google Ads scripts for bulk bid adjustments, budget alerts, and scheduled pausing. Use Grok for real-time trend research on seasonal campaign angles.
Perplexity · Claude
Keyword Research
Research search intent patterns and emerging product queries. Structure keyword lists by funnel stage and match type, reducing manual grouping time significantly.
Boundary Setting
Where AI Doesn't Replace Me
Final bid strategy decisions, audience qualification for high-budget clients, and stakeholder communication stay fully human. AI handles volume — I handle judgement.
Honest note: Every AI output — copy, analysis, or brief — gets reviewed before it touches a live campaign or client. AI tools accelerate the work; they don't replace the thinking.

Google Ads · Performance Max

Acer Dotcom Network: Multi-Location Performance Max

Scaled PMax campaigns across the Acer Dotcom store network — Airport, Marina Mall, Mountroad, Annanagar, Velachery, Adyar — to generate high-volume conversions at controlled CPA. All figures from campaign report.

Situation
Multiple Acer Dotcom store locations running isolated campaigns with fragmented budgets, no shared audience signals, and inconsistent bid strategies. Conversion volumes varied significantly.
Action
Restructured to individual PMax campaigns per location with store-specific asset groups, GA4 audience signals from past converters, and unified Target CPA bidding. Creative A/B tests every 4 weeks.
Results (from report)
Acer-Microtouch: 3,812 conv, ₹18,031 spend, 3.65% CTR. Acer-AumTech Aug: 2,265 conv, 5.43% CTR, ₹0.27 CPC. Multiple stores at ₹0.25–₹0.38 CPC at high volume.
3,812 conv — single store 5.43% peak CTR ₹0.27 CPC achieved Target CPA bidding
Google Search · Policy-Restricted

Indian Craft Brewery: Search Campaign Architecture

Drove measurable conversions for ICB through Google Search in a policy-restricted hospitality and beverage category. Data directly from campaign report.

Situation
Alcohol-adjacent brand facing Google Ads policy restrictions. Multiple ad disapprovals, limited targeting options, need to drive restaurant and brewery footfall without violating policies.
Action
Built 6 intent-segmented Search campaigns: Brewery, Restaurant, Cocktails, Dinner, Lunch, Generic, Near Me. Compliant messaging on dining experience. Call extensions and location assets applied. Separate PMax for broader reach.
Results (from report)
ICB Search Brewery: 16,926 conv, ₹1,01,385 spend, 11.46% CTR. ICB PMax Feb-25: 1,09,923 conv, ₹3,50,792 spend, 5.24% CTR. ICB 2026 PMax: 27,487 conv, ₹1,36,210 spend.
16,926 conv (Search only) 11.46% CTR 1,09,923 conv (PMax) 6 intent segments

Lenovo APAC · Google Business Profile

Lenovo APAC SPOC: 250+ GBP Profiles, +15% Discoverability

As Lenovo's APAC Single Point of Contact, managed and optimised 250+ Google Business Profile listings across partner retail outlets, improving local search discoverability by 15%.

Situation
250+ partner store GBP listings with inconsistent NAP data, low photo quality, poor review response rates, and missing service attributes — reducing local map pack visibility.
Action
Audited all 250+ listings for completeness, review rate, and category accuracy. Prioritised bottom 20% for remediation. Built a GBP optimisation playbook: standardised NAP data, keyword-rich descriptions, structured service attributes, monthly photo cadence.
Results
+15% local search discoverability across APAC markets. Higher qualified store footfall. Repeatable GBP playbook adopted across all 250+ partner locations.
250+ profiles managed +15% discoverability APAC multi-market GBP playbook built
