The Challenge

ICB is an alcohol-adjacent hospitality brand — multiple ad disapprovals, strict Google Ads policy restrictions, and zero prior campaign structure. Needed to drive restaurant and brewery footfall across dining, cocktails, brewery and near-me intents simultaneously without violating policies.

Situation
No compliant campaign structure. Multiple disapprovals. All intents (brewery, dinner, cocktails, near me) mixed together. Quality scores low, spend wasted.
Action
Built 6 intent-segmented Search campaigns — Brewery, Restaurant, Cocktails, Dinner, Lunch, Near Me. Ad copy focused on dining experience. PMax layer for broader reach.
Result
ICB_Search_Brewery_Beer: 16,926 conv, 11.46% CTR, ₹5.99 CPA. ICB PMax Feb-25: 1,09,923 conv, ₹3.19 CPA. ICB 2026 PMax: 27,487 conv.

The Challenge

10+ Acer Dotcom and partner store locations across Chennai & Bengaluru — Airport, T Nagar, Mountroad, Marina Mall, Velachery, VR Mall Annanagar, Adyar, Microtouch, AumTech — each with separate budgets, no shared audience signals, and inconsistent CPCs across campaigns.

Situation
Fragmented per-location campaigns with no GA4 audience signals. High CPC variation. No creative consistency. Budgets running independently with no shared learning.
Action
Individual PMax per outlet with store-specific asset groups, GA4 past-convertor signals, Target CPA bidding. Negative keyword lists and A/B creative tests refreshed every 4 weeks.
Result
Microtouch Aug'24: 3,812 conv, ₹4.73 CPA, ₹0.38 CPC. Airport Nov'24: 3,182 conv, 5.46% CTR, ₹0.25 CPC. T Nagar Oct'25: 3,649 conv, 6.29% CTR.

The Challenge

As Lenovo's APAC SPOC, inherited 250+ Google Business Profile listings across partner retail outlets in Southeast Asia and the broader APAC region — inconsistent NAP data, missing attributes, low review engagement suppressing local map pack visibility. Separately tasked with running paid Google Ads campaigns (PMax) on Lenovo's APAC store locator — locate.lenovo.com — targeting the Philippines market for awareness and store locator traffic.

Situation
250+ Lenovo APAC listings with inconsistent NAP data, missing attributes, low review engagement — suppressing local map pack visibility across APAC markets.
Action
Full GBP audit across all 250+ listings. Built standardisation playbook — NAP alignment, keyword-rich descriptions, service attributes, monthly photo cadence, review response templates. Launched PMax on locate.lenovo.com targeting Philippines for store locator awareness.
Result
+15% increase in APAC local discoverability. GBP playbook adopted across all locations. Philippines PMax campaign drove measurable traffic to Lenovo's store locator.
