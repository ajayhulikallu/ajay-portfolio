AI in Practice

How I use AI in real campaigns

Not automation for its own sake — using the right tool to save time, improve output, and move faster.

ChatGPT · Claude
Ad Copy & Brief Generation
Use ChatGPT and Claude to generate 10–15 headline variants per ad group in minutes. Test them against each other in Google Ads, identify winners faster, and iterate based on real CTR data rather than guesswork.
Claude · Perplexity
Campaign Analysis & Insights
Paste campaign CSVs into Claude to surface underperforming ad groups, identify audience overlap issues, and generate client-ready summaries. Use Perplexity for quick competitor research without 10 tabs open.
Gamma · ChatGPT
Reporting & Presentations
Use ChatGPT to draft performance commentary from raw metrics. Use Gamma to convert briefs into structured decks for client QBRs — reducing presentation prep time by ~30% without compromising quality.
ChatGPT · Grok
Automation Scripting
Use ChatGPT to write Google Ads scripts for bulk bid adjustments, budget alerts, and scheduled pausing. Use Grok for real-time trend research when planning seasonal campaign angles.
Perplexity · Claude
Keyword Research & Strategy
Use Perplexity to research search intent patterns and emerging product queries. Use Claude to structure keyword lists by funnel stage and match type, reducing manual grouping time significantly.
All Tools
Where I Don't Rely on AI
Final bid strategy decisions, audience qualification for high-budget clients, and APAC stakeholder communication stay fully human. AI handles volume tasks — I handle judgement calls.
Honest note: I use AI tools to move faster, not to replace thinking. Every output — copy, analysis, or brief — gets reviewed before it goes anywhere near a live campaign or client.

Google Ads · Performance Max

Acer Dotcom Network: Multi-Location Performance Max Scale-Up

Scaled Performance Max campaigns across the Acer Dotcom store network (Airport, Marina Mall, Mountroad, Annanagar, Velachery, Adyar and more) to generate high-volume conversions at controlled CPA. All figures directly from campaign report.

🔍 Situation
Multiple Acer Dotcom store locations running isolated campaigns with fragmented budgets, no shared audience signals, and inconsistent bid strategies. Conversion volumes varied significantly across locations.
⚙️ Action
Restructured to individual PMax campaigns per location with store-specific asset groups, GA4 audience signals from past converters, and unified Target CPA bidding. Applied negative keyword exclusions at account level and ran creative A/B tests every 4 weeks.
📈 Results (from report)
Acer-Microtouch Infotech: 3,812 conversions, ₹18,031 spend, ₹4.73 CPA, 3.65% CTR. Acer-5 Computers Aug'24: 2,152 conversions, ₹18,039 spend. Acer-AumTech Aug'24: 2,265 conversions, 5.43% CTR.
3,812 conv · single campaign ₹4.73 CPA achieved 5.43% peak CTR ₹0.27–0.38 CPC on high-volume stores
Google Search · Policy-Restricted Category

Indian Craft Brewery: Search Campaign Architecture in Policy-Heavy Category

Drove measurable conversions and footfall for ICB through Google Search in a policy-restricted hospitality/beverage category. Data from campaign report only.

🔍 Situation
Alcohol-adjacent brand facing Google Ads policy restrictions. Multiple ad disapprovals, limited targeting options, need to drive restaurant and brewery footfall without violating platform policies.
⚙️ Action
Built 6 segmented Search campaigns by intent: Brewery, Restaurant, Cocktails, Dinner, Lunch, Generic, and Near Me. Compliant messaging focused on dining experience. Applied call extensions and location assets. Separate PMax campaign for broader reach.
📈 Results (from report)
ICB_Search_Brewery: 16,926 conversions, ₹1,01,385 spend, 11.46% CTR. ICB-PMax-Feb-25: 1,09,923 conversions, ₹3,50,792 spend, 5.24% CTR. ICB 2026 PMax: 27,487 conversions, ₹1,36,210 spend.
16,926 conv (Search alone) 11.46% CTR on Brewery Search 1,09,923 conv on PMax 5.24% PMax CTR
Shopify · CRO · GA4

Britannia Shopify Store: +11% Conversion Rate in 90 Days

Built Britannia's Shopify e-commerce store and improved overall conversion rate by 11% within 90 days through GA4 funnel analysis and systematic CRO changes.

🔍 Situation
New Shopify store with no GA4 tracking, high checkout drop-off rates, and generic landing pages not optimised for product-specific intent. Conversion rate was at baseline with no measurement infrastructure.
⚙️ Action
Set up full GA4 + GTM tracking (scroll depth, add-to-cart, checkout steps). Used GA4 funnel exploration to identify 62% drop-off between product page and cart. Hypothesised 3 causes: page speed, CTA placement, insufficient social proof. Ran 2-week A/B test per change.
📈 Results
+11% conversion rate improvement within 90 days. Full-funnel visibility established via GA4 and GTM. Identified winning CTA position and layout — CTA moved above fold, product images compressed, review widgets added near price.
+11% conversion rate lift 90-day delivery GA4 full-funnel tracking built A/B test validated
Lenovo APAC · Google Business Profile

Lenovo APAC SPOC: 250+ GBP Profiles · +15% Local Discoverability

As the designated APAC Single Point of Contact for Lenovo, managed and optimised 250+ Google Business Profile listings across partner retail outlets, improving local search discoverability by 15%.

🔍 Situation
250+ partner store GBP listings with inconsistent NAP data, low photo quality, poor review response rates, and missing service attributes — resulting in low local map pack visibility and reduced qualified footfall.
⚙️ Action
Audited all 250+ listings for completeness score, review rate, and category accuracy. Prioritised bottom 20% for immediate remediation. Built a GBP optimisation playbook: standardised NAP data, keyword-rich descriptions, structured service attributes, monthly photo refresh cadence.
📈 Results
+15% improvement in local search discoverability across APAC markets. Drove higher qualified store footfall to Lenovo partner outlets. Established repeatable playbook adopted across all 250+ locations.
250+ profiles managed +15% local discoverability APAC multi-market scope GBP playbook created
