Google Ads · PMax

Acer Dotcom Multi-Location PMax Scale-Up

Scaled Performance Max campaigns across 10+ Acer Dotcom store locations to deliver high-volume conversions at controlled CPA.

Multiple Acer Dotcom stores running isolated, low-performing campaigns with inconsistent bidding, budget cannibalization, and no shared audience intelligence. CPA was volatile across the network.
Consolidated location-level learnings into a PMax framework with shared signals. Applied Target CPA and Target ROAS per store tier. Built audience lists from GA4 — past converters, high-intent visitors — as PMax signals.
Separate PMax campaigns per location with hyper-local asset groups. GTM + GA4 for precise conversion tracking. A/B tested creatives every 4 weeks. Applied negative keyword exclusions at account level.

Meta Ads · Hospitality

Meta Full-Funnel for Event & Hospitality Brands

Built and refined Meta campaigns for hospitality clients, improving CTR by 18% through creative testing and funnel segmentation.

Campaigns running with broad targeting and no creative differentiation between cold and warm audiences. Rebuilt the funnel: TOF used broad + lookalike audiences, MOF used engagement retargeting, BOF used website visitor retargeting with urgency creatives.
3-variant tests: (A) static image, (B) short video testimonial, (C) carousel USP breakdown. Video outperformed static by 2.3x on engagement. Scaled budget 60% toward top performers, reducing wasted impressions by 35%.
